About Us

Corvus is a payroll software automation, to improve SMEs efficiency. For accountants looking to automate and control their service and companies that need to automate their payroll processes. Start keeping your company's accounting in a centralized (on your phone) and simplified way (move and see the data you need in one click). Your bisiness data in one place.

While in the technology epicenter regions in the globe, new services have reached the point of competing in producing higher and higher percentages of efficiency over maximum percentages. In Latin America, although it is not the goal to even make agriculture an automated and robotized affair. It is obvious that even basic computational issues such as data control, payroll or cash flow have not yet been implemented, although they are necessary to improve the production of both companies and society in general.

To make payroll processes more technological is a leap that many entrepreneurs still hesitate or would like to carry out in their companies, but they are afraid or have had bad experiences in learning these new, but increasingly simple ways to control these processes.

Corvus offers a secure bridge to all these users and emerging entrepreneurs, too. To make the control of all their payroll data easier, no matter how many locations and employees are in your company. And it is especially dedicated to accounting and business administration professionals who want to digitize their customers' portfolios. To free yourself from administrative burdens and repetitive processes in your payroll services.
